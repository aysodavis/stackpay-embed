AYSO United Davis Swag Ordering

Each year, we create a special, limited production run of Davis United gear—just for our community! This summer, we’re offering our classic “Davis is United” outerwear alongside a favorite design chosen by players.

🗓️ Order Deadline: July 20
Orders will be fulfilled locally by Ink Monkey. All taxes and fees are included in the price below.

Pick-up will happen at our season kick-off event on August 14 or with your affiliated team afterward. If you’re not affiliated with a team or ordering from afar (cannot pick-up in Davis), no problem—just send us an email at aysouniteddavis@aysouniteddavis.org and we’ll organize a convenient pickup option for you.

These designs are exclusive to our Davis soccer community and won’t be available again this soccer season—don’t miss out on your chance to show your Davis United pride!
